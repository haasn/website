libswscale reimagined

Talk
VideoLAN Dev Days 2025 November 2025

An overview of my project for the Sovereign Tech Fund 2024, which was all about rewriting/refactoring libswscale. I went into depth explaining my novel approach to pixel format conversion, which was based around generating and optimizing an abstract operation list and then "JIT" compiling this by combining a list of assembly fragments using continuation passing style.

Paper
Ulm University August 2017

Bachelor's Thesis. Comparative study of three Bitcoin network simulators (ns-3, Shadow, and simbit), evaluating their user-friendliness, performance characteristics, adaptability to other cryptocurrencies, and range of configurable parameters. Provides detailed performance benchmarks and recommendations.
