Hello, I'm Niklas Haas

I'm an independent IT consultant based in Germany, specializing in multimedia processing, color management, and high-performance optimization.

With nearly 20 years of active participation in the open source multimedia community, I bring deep technical expertise and a proven track record of solving complex problems in video processing, color management, and high-performance computing.

I am a regular contributor to industry-leading projects including FFmpeg, libplacebo, VLC, and mpv, where I focus on advancing the state of the art in multimedia processing and playback.

Skills & Expertise

Deep technical expertise across the full stack of multimedia processing, from low-level hardware optimization to high-level algorithm design.

GPU Programming & Graphics

Expert in GPU-accelerated computing with extensive experience in Vulkan and OpenGL. Proficient in shader programming (GLSL/HLSL) for real-time graphics and compute workloads. Specialized in designing efficient GPU pipelines for image and video processing.

Performance Optimization

Specialist in low-level performance optimization using SIMD instruction sets including x86 SSE, AVX2, AVX512, and RISC-V Vector (RVV). Expert in profiling, analyzing, and optimizing performance-critical code paths for maximum throughput.

SIMD AVX2/AVX512 RISC-V Vector Assembly

Color Management & HDR

Deep expertise in color science, HDR workflows, and advanced tone mapping techniques. Developer of tone mapping algorithms in active use across the industry. Experienced with Dolby Vision and various HDR standards for broadcast and consumer applications.

HDR Dolby Vision Tone Mapping Color Science

Multimedia & Image Processing

Extensive experience with video codecs, digital image processing algorithms, and processing pipelines. Proficient in both traditional DSP techniques and modern ML-based approaches including neural network image processing.
